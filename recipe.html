Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish with a rich and savory meat sauce — perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Finished Dish

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 300g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add onions and garlic, and sauté until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Drain spaghetti, top with sauce, and serve hot.

Tips

Pro Tip: For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering. You can also reserve some pasta water to adjust sauce consistency.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)
